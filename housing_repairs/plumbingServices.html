Plumbing Services

We provide professional plumbing solutions for homes and small businesses. Choose a service below and click Book Now to request a quote.

Leak detection & repairs

Our team uses visual inspection and pipe-tracing tools to locate leaks quickly. We repair or replace damaged pipe sections and test the system to ensure there are no further leaks. Work includes minor welding, seal replacement and pressure testing where needed.

Burst pipes & geysers

For burst pipes we provide emergency response to stop water flow and limit damage. For geysers, we perform safety checks, pressure relief valve inspections and full system testing after repair or replacement.

Drain unblocking

We clear blocked drains using manual and mechanical methods. We use: high-pressure water jetting, and camera inspection for persistent blockages. After clearing, we run a full flush and advise on preventative maintenance.

Bathroom & Kitchen Installations

We install sinks, taps, toilets, showers, and related plumbing for bathroom and kitchen renovations. Work includes accurate measurement, secure pipe connections, water-tight testing and final finish checks to ensure reliability and a clean installation.
