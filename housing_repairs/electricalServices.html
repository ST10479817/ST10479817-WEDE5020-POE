Our Services

Fault finding & repair

We use diagnostic tools and testing to trace electrical faults. Common fixes include replacing faulty wiring, breakers or fuses and securing loose connections. All repairs comply with safety standards to restore reliable power.

Lighting installations

From basic ceiling lights to modern LED systems, we install and connect all types of lighting. Services include wiring, switch connection and final safety checks.

Socket & switch installations

Whether adding extra outlets or upgrading existing ones, we ensure safe installation with proper earthing and circuit testing. Our team installs both standard and modern USB sockets, as well as single switches.

Backup power setup

We set up reliable backup systems, including generators, inverters and UPS connections. Installation includes safe changeover switches, proper load balancing and system testing so your home or business stays powered during outages.
